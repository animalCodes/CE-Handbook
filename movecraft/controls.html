Controlling your ship

So, you've got your hands on a spaceship, neat. But how do you actually move it? That's what this page is for.


There is three main ways to control your ship: With a compass, 'Direct Control', and signs.

Compass control

This is the main way you will control your ship, and also arguably the simplest. (is that a word? it is now) All you need to do is grab a compass (you should already have one), pilot your ship, and right-click with your compass in the direction you want to go, easy!
